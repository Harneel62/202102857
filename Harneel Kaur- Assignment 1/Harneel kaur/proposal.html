Complaint Management System Proposal

Proposal:

Our complaint management system aims to provide users a system for submitting, tracking, and resolving complaints. Users can easily submit their complaints online, track the status of their complaints, and communicate with the relevant authorities regarding their issues.

Features:

  • User registration and authentication
  • Submission of complaints with relevant details
  • Tracking of the complaints to check the progress and status
  • Add notes on complaints by diffrent authorities
  • updates on complaint status
  • Analytics and reporting for to see what type of complaints are submitted

User Interface:

The user interface will consist of:

  • User registration and login pages
  • Complaint submission form with fields for description, category, priority, etc.
  • Dashboard for users to track their submitted complaints and view updates

Database Requirements:

The database will store information such as:

  • User details (name, email, password, etc.)
  • Complaint details (description, category, status, priority, timestamps, etc.)
  • Assigned departments or individuals for handling complaints
  • Notes that are added on a complaint n the process

This complaint management system will eliiminate the need to manually storing complaints and make them more secure and accessible to the complainant.
